NAME
herbs and mixtures - common mixtures and how to make them

SYNOPSIS
mix '<spell name>' <quantity>

DESCRIPTION
Herbalism is a powerful skill that enables the herbalist to create
magical powders. Different herbs of varying strengths are combined to
form mixtures using the MIX command and a mortar and pestle.

Mortar and Pestle

The mortar and pestle is the most basic, but very important, part of
creating mixtures.  A basic granite mortar and pestle can be commonly
found in numerous locations throughout Alyria, including in the great
Druids' city of Xaventry, and can be used by beginning herbalists.
Experienced herbalists may obtain other, more powerful magic mortars
in their travels.  These magical mortar and pestles can also increase
the power of the mixture and this additional power must be taken into
account when making calculations.

Sprigs
Sprigs are no longer necessary to mix herbal mixtures.
